## Target Market Analysis

### Primary Target Segments

#### 1. **Wholesale Distributors** (Segment A)
**Profile**:
- Company size: 5-50 employees
- Revenue: $500k - $10M annually
- Pain: Managing inventory across multiple warehouses, manual order processing
- Current tools: Excel, QuickBooks, or legacy ERP that's too complex

**Why They Need This**:
- ✅ High inventory turnover (need real-time stock tracking)
- ✅ Multi-warehouse support
- ✅ Vendor/customer relationship management
- ✅ Integration with accounting (QuickBooks/Xero)

**Estimated Market Size**: 150,000+ businesses in the US alone

---

#### 2. **Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) Brands** (Segment B)
**Profile**:
- Company size: 3-20 employees
- Revenue: $200k - $5M annually
- Pain: Managing Shopify inventory + manual purchase orders + disconnected systems
- Current tools: Shopify + Google Sheets + Stripe

**Why They Need This**:
- ✅ Shopify integration (auto-sync inventory)
- ✅ Purchase order management (supplier relationships)
- ✅ Cost tracking (know true profit margins)
- ✅ Forecasting (prevent stockouts during campaigns)

**Estimated Market Size**: 500,000+ Shopify stores with > $100k revenue

---

#### 3. **Service-Based Businesses with Inventory** (Segment C)
**Profile**:
- HVAC, plumbing, electrical contractors
- Company size: 10-100 employees
- Pain: Tracking parts/materials used per job, vendor management

**Why They Need This**:
- ✅ Job-based inventory allocation
- ✅ Mobile app for field technicians
- ✅ Vendor bill management

**Estimated Market Size**: 300,000+ businesses in the US

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

| Plan | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Free** | $0/month | Solo founders, testing | 1 user, 50 products, 25 orders/month, basic reports |
| **Starter** | $49/month | Small teams | 3 users, unlimited products/orders, 1 warehouse, email support |
| **Professional** | $149/month | Growing businesses | 10 users, multi-warehouse, workflow automation, integrations, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large teams | Unlimited users, dedicated instance, custom integrations, SLA, phone support |

**Add-Ons** (à la carte):
- **Extra Users**: $10/user/month (above plan limit)
- **AI Forecasting**: $29/month (predictive inventory alerts)
- **Advanced Integrations**: $19/month per connector (Shopify, QuickBooks, etc.)
- **White-Label**: $299/month (remove branding, custom domain)

### Pricing Psychology

1. **Free Tier**: Attracts users, gets them hooked, converts 3-5% to paid
2. **Starter at $49**: Cheaper than most ERPs ($200+/month), easier to say "yes"
3. **Professional at $149**: Sweet spot for businesses earning $1M+/year (0.15% of revenue)
4. **Enterprise**: For businesses ready to pay for dedicated support and SLAs

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) & Lifetime Value (LTV)

### Target Metrics (Healthy SaaS)

| Metric | Target | Formula |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **CAC** | < $150 | (Sales + Marketing Spend) / New Customers |
| **LTV** | > $1,500 | (ARPU × Gross Margin) / Churn Rate |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | > 3:1 | LTV / CAC |
| **Payback Period** | < 12 months | CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) |

### Example Calculation (Month 12)

**Assumptions**:
- Marketing spend: $10,000/month
- New customers: 50/month
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User): $100/month
- Gross margin: 85% (SaaS standard)
- Churn rate: 8%/month (8% of customers cancel each month)

**Calculations**:
- **CAC**: $10,000 / 50 = **$200**
- **LTV**: ($100 × 0.85) / 0.08 = **$1,062**
- **LTV:CAC**: $1,062 / $200 = **5.3:1** ✅ (Excellent)
- **Payback Period**: $200 / ($100 × 0.85) = **2.4 months** ✅ (Very good)

## Churn Reduction Strategy

### Why Customers Churn

| Reason | % of Churn | Mitigation |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Didn't see value** | 40% | Better onboarding, feature discovery |
| **Too expensive** | 20% | Flexible pricing, annual discounts |
| **Missing features** | 20% | Feature request pipeline, fast iteration |
| **Competitor** | 10% | Competitive monitoring, match critical features |
| **Business closed** | 10% | Unavoidable |

### Retention Tactics

1. **Onboarding Checklist**:
   - "Complete these 5 steps to get 80% value"
   - Step 1: Add 10 products
   - Step 2: Create your first sales order
   - Step 3: Generate an invoice
   - Step 4: Set up a workflow automation
   - Step 5: Invite your team
2. **In-App Engagement**:
   - Show feature tips based on usage patterns
   - "You haven't used Reports yet. Here's how to track your revenue."
3. **Customer Success Check-Ins**:
   - Month 1: Automated email (how's it going?)
   - Month 3: Personal call from founder/CSM
   - Month 6: Quarterly business review (QBR)
4. **Annual Billing Incentive**:
   - Offer 2 months free (save 17%) for annual payment
   - Reduces churn (harder to cancel when prepaid)
